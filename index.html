Crystal Collector

Game Instructions:


  1. The target number (or random number) is given at the beginning of the game"generated number"
  2. Click on any of the colored crystals.
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
  3. Click on the gems to add to your score
  4. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
  5. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  6. Each crystal has a different value, so choose carefully!
  7. The crystals are never the same so be careful.
